Frequently Asked Questions

How do you choose products to review?

We select products based on reader requests, market popularity, and our expertise in specific categories. We also consider products that offer unique value propositions or address common consumer needs.

Do you accept products for free reviews?

We primarily purchase products ourselves to maintain independence. However, we occasionally accept products for review from established brands, always with full disclosure and no obligation to provide positive reviews.

How long does it take to review a product?

Our comprehensive reviews typically take 2-4 weeks to complete, depending on the product category. This allows for thorough testing in various real-world scenarios.

Can I suggest a product for review?

Absolutely! We value our community's input. Use the contact form above or email us with your product suggestions. Popular requests often influence our review schedule.
